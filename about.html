The Founder's Vision: Vivian

[Image of Vivian placeholder]

GiFTED was born from Vivian's profound belief in the untapped potential of Nigerian talent. Growing up, Vivian witnessed firsthand the incredible creativity, resilience, and ingenuity that often went unnoticed or lacked global avenues for expression.

Her vision transcended mere observation; she envisioned a dynamic ecosystem where Nigerian artisans, innovators, artists, and entrepreneurs could not only hone their craft but also connect with international markets, investors, and mentors. Vivian's journey began with a singular mission: to build a bridge between local brilliance and global opportunity.

Driven by a passion for empowerment and a deep understanding of the unique challenges faced by emerging talents, Vivian meticulously crafted the GiFTED framework. Her dedication to fostering sustainable growth, equitable partnerships, and celebrating cultural heritage is the cornerstone of everything GiFTED stands for. It’s more than a brand; it’s a movement sparked by one woman’s unwavering faith in her people.

Our Mission, Vision & Core Values

Our Mission

To identify, empower, and promote exceptional Nigerian talents across diverse creative and innovative industries, connecting them to global platforms and opportunities.

Our Vision

To be the leading global hub for discovering and showcasing Nigerian brilliance, fostering a sustainable ecosystem that drives economic prosperity and cultural exchange.

Core Values

  • Excellence: Upholding the highest standards in everything we do.
  • Integrity: Operating with transparency, honesty, and ethical conduct.
  • Empowerment: Providing tools and opportunities for growth and self-sufficiency.
  • Innovation: Embracing creativity and forward-thinking solutions.
  • Community: Building a supportive and collaborative environment.

Our Impact

GiFTED is committed to creating measurable positive change within Nigeria and on the global stage. Our initiatives are designed to:

  • Boost Local Economies: By creating job opportunities and fostering entrepreneurial growth.
  • Promote Cultural Heritage: Showcasing unique Nigerian artistry and innovation to the world.
  • Facilitate Global Connections: Bridging the gap between local talents and international markets/partners.
  • Inspire Future Generations: Providing role models and pathways for aspiring talents.
  • Drive Sustainable Development: Investing in long-term programs that ensure lasting impact.

We believe in the power of talent to transform lives and communities, and every project we undertake is a step towards realizing that potential.
